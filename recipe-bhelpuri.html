Bhel Puri

A popular Indian street snack made with puffed rice, vegetables, and tangy chutneys. Light, crunchy, and full of flavor!

ü•ó Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled and diced potatoes
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp green chutney
  • 1/2 cup sev
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt to taste

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add puffed rice, onions, tomatoes, and boiled potatoes.
  2. Add tamarind chutney, green chutney, and mix well.
  3. Sprinkle chaat masala and salt as per taste.
  4. Just before serving, add sev and coriander. Toss lightly.
  5. Serve immediately for best crunch and taste.
